About Dash


At Dash, our journey began in 2010 with a shared vision and relentless passion for digital innovation. Founded by Emmanuel Benson, our agency emerged from collaborative efforts with a common goal: to revolutionize the way businesses interact and thrive online. Inspired by the rapidly evolving digital landscape, we embarked on a journey to create a web agency like no other. From humble beginnings in a small office space to our current status as a leading force in the industry, our founding story is a testament to perseverance, creativity, and unwavering dedication.


Dash provides a wide range of premium digital consultancy services to small and medium-sized businesses. Put simply, we generate more traffic, leads, sales, and time for our customers. The Dash team has worked with a wide variety of clients ranging from large corporate blue-chip companies to medium and small enterprises. We offer several decades of experience and expertise to help solve your business problems with digital solutions. We guarantee that each client gets the same careful attention to ensure their online presence matches their unique business needs and expectations.


Today, as we look back on our journey, we're proud to have helped countless businesses achieve their online goals and realize their full potential. Our founding story is not just a reflection of our past—it's a blueprint for our future, as we continue to push the boundaries of possibility and shape the digital landscape for years to come.
