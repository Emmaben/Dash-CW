Meet the Team, get to know the talented individuals behind Dash


Meet the dynamic individuals who make up our talented team at Dash. Comprised of passionate designers, skilled developers, and dedicated project managers, our team is united by a shared commitment to excellence and innovation. With diverse backgrounds and expertise spanning various disciplines within the digital realm, we collaborate seamlessly to bring creative visions to life and deliver exceptional results for our clients.


Each member of our team brings a unique perspective and skill set to the table, contributing to our collective success and fostering a culture of collaboration, creativity, and continuous learning. Get to know the faces behind the projects and discover the people who are dedicated to turning your ideas into reality.

We Create Dynamic Growth We create innovation that inspires


Our team was originally part of a highly successful E-commerce group which build several 7 figure E-commerce business from the ground up before a successful exit in 2019.

We came into fruition by running our E-commerce companies which puts us in a very unique position in comparison to almost all other agencies. After our exit we were able to retain our in house team and create the Dash in order to offer our world class expertise to other businesses.

Our knowledge goes far beyond simply technology, marketing and creative and we are consider ourselves a core part of all of our clients strategic ambitions. We work with you as a partner in helping you achieve your goals.
